Hi!

I’m an Interaction and Service Designer with an academic background in Asian Studies and extensive experience in the field of filmmaking and motion design.

Ethnographic research, storytelling and creative technologies are the main tools that I bring into my design process, to explore people’s needs and prototype products and services.

This approach extends also to the installations I produce with Polisonum, an art collective focused on acoustic ecology and sound art.
